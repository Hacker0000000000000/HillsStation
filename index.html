Dandoba Hills Station

Dandoba Bhose
The Dandoba Hill Forest Preserve can be reached with a 25 minute drive from Sangli.
This forest preserve is rich in flora and fauna and also has a few historically significant ancient temples in its hills. The hills of this preserve are a popular location for trekking and long hikes. There is also an annual mini-marathon race held at Dandoba Hill Station, which is quite a popular event.
